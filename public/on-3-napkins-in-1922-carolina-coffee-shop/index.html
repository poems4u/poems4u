On 3 Napkins in 1922 Carolina Coffee Shop

N.1 If all was light, we wouldn’t be able to see. I will go where I am led and through this giving thanks, may I be brought into what I would always become, and may my heart quicken in gladness at the thought of doing it again. N.2 If it’s true that the two primary emotions are fear and love, and that they are mutually exclusive, Then maybe FDR did nothing wrong, and to be afraid is to let the evil of the world enter your heart. It would like to make you believe you have everything to lose; nothing could be further from the truth. N.3 If, in the end, mediocrity reigns, then may I be exceptional or awful. If, in the time passing, philosophy was spake through literature, then may I live a life of art with no writing or speaking.
