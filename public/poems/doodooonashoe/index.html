Old and weathered,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Muddy ground giving way,

Truths held as fact until proven wrong,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Horde of gold into dirt,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Dirt into gold
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Spat upon,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Cast out,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Shat upon,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Beaten,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Abused,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Objectified,
\nbsp;     nbsp;     \nbsp;     nbsp;     \ Internalized,

No water, no sun,
New moon, no light, Rock, wind, debris;
Where else but the navel with your sight?
