Hue and Cry Over Kronstadt

Dear, Abby

“What do a woman and a plane have in common?”

Isn’t it strange what we choose to make into spectacles?

Abstraction is your friend!
So is mystery.
Blurry…

An essay can be a poem.
A memoir cannot.

An aphorism and an ever present question,

         “What would you do
              if when you okay
                  so he said yes would go?”
