Proteins swirling, <br>  
Giggling, churning 

Whirled them East, <br>  
Ever East——the summit 

They scaled and climbed <br>  
And wrote and rhymed

There way up <br>  
Why, can they only see <br>  
Senator Long's Body <br>  
Still, a-mouldering:

Nine Angels count the difference.
