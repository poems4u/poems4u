I saw a mockingbird chase a squirrel up a tree, <br>  
Mammal scritting, avian flitting, <br>  
Its feet scratching the bark, its wings displacing the air, <br>  
Who resents the most: <br>  
Being chased, or being made to chase, <br>  
And was the oak even disturbed? <br>  

It was said <br>  
“Two is better than one, and four is better than two.” <br>  
Agreement notwithstanding, <br>  
It was said, and now it’s done. <br>  

The sky relishes its orange hue, <br>  
While the moon, obscured, can only fume. <br>  
She was born a smoker, <br>  
And her pocked skin resembles you. <br>  

Renverser l’ordre des choses, <br>  
C’est une autre chose entièrement, <br>  
Than what came to be and passed away. 

You ordered steak frites, please, <br>  
And eat while <br>  
My stomach churns, <br>  
The world burns, <br>  
Shall I again feel at ease?
