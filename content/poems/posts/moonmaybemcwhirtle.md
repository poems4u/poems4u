Mourning dove up above, <br>  
Her bosom shines on earth; <br>  
Her haughty full-ish-ness <br>  
Gone in six days <br>  

To be seen soon again, <br>  
Without apology; <br>  
She has needs, also yours <br>  
Are here displayed. <br>
